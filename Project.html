How Our Community Works

Our community platform is the heart of CCLA, where international students share real experiences and practical advice. Browse posts from other users about life in Yekaterinburg, and publish your own insights to help newcomers.

  • Browse Experience Posts - Read about accommodation, food, transportation, and university life from real students
  • Publish Your Own Stories - Share your experiences about local scenic spots, restaurants, hotels, and stores
