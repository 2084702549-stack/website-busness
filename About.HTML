Our Mission & Services

Empowering Your Global Journey

CCLA is dedicated to providing essential digital assistance to international students, tourists, and business visitors, ensuring a smooth and confident transition to life in Russia. We are your reliable partner in navigating cultural differences and local procedures.

What We Offer

  • Translation & Official Guidance

    We provide comprehensive location guidance and real-time translation for official procedures like university enrollment, visa extension, and mandatory medical checkups. We simplify complexity into clear, actionable steps.

  • Peer-to-Peer Community Platform

    Our vibrant community allows users to share authentic reviews and experiences on local businesses, scenic spots, accommodation, and hotels. Get trusted advice from fellow students and travelers.

  • Smart Cloud Shopping Assistant

    Use our smart feature to scan product price tags in partnered supermarkets. Instantly receive product details, language translation, price comparisons, and view related deals.
